About Bryan Cam

Bryan is an LA-based voice actor witht the ability to provide audio via Source Connect, IdPTL, Skype, or commuting to a studio out in LA. His voice ranges from warm and calming to sassy, brash, and heroic. There isn't a voice Bryan hasn't tried to jump into the shoes of! One of his best traits is how resilient he is and how he can take direction.

Bryan grew interest about the voice acting industry from watching shows like Ben 10 and Teen Titans. He often found himself intrigued how an individual could go from a dark brooding character to someone so vibrant and adventurous. He then would find himself observing that we all have "characters" that we would go to, when put in different situations/circumstances. He started the ground running back in 2015 by researching what studios look for in a voice actor and how to get your feet wet. He then began taking commercial training in order to get used to reading script and taking directions, while at the same time getting used to microphone techniques.

Bryan then began his animation and character voice acting lessons in 2018 and fell in love with the act of playing pretend. He often would be inspired from workshops, that he attends, to see how others would have their portrayal on a script/character. As a person that grew up watching shows and playing games, he feels comfort in being able to express himself within the script that he's reading.

When he isn't doing voice acting related activities, you can find Bryan browsing on yelp or nearby restaurants for food, being that he is an absolute foodie at heart. If he isn't doing that, he's also watching shows and going to the movies to keep up to date or draw inspiration from the media and how actors portray certain characters. If you really know Bryan, he is a huge karaoke fanatic and loves singing. You'll catch him singing or humming a tune once in awhile if you spend enough time with him.
