About Bryan Cam

Bryan is an LA-based voice actor. He was first intrigued about voice acting at a young age and grew up watching shows like Ben 10 and Teen Titans. When he found out the characters of Ben and Raven were voiced by the same person he was mind boggled.

Inspired, Bryan would often try out a variety of characters and voices to match the different situations he found himself in. These voices form the repertoire he uses to this day.

He started his commercial voice acting training in 2015 at Big House Casting with Kate McClanaghan. After taking a couple of classes he was ready to produce his demo with Susan Playo. With the commercial demo completed, Bryan had to decide whether he wanted to go straight into pay to play sites or whether he should still take classes for animation and character development.

Bryan took some time exploring the voiceover industry, taking advantage of any and all opportunities to develop himself. He began taking animation and character voice acting classes in 2018. He first started off with Tony Oliver's Intermediate and Advanced workshop. After falling in love with the process of discovering characters in voice acting, he moved on to classes with Richard Horvitz, Crispin Freeman, Julie Kliewer, Roy Yokelson, and David Rosenthal.

His mentors and coaches praise him for his "warm and smiling" commercial takes. His animation coaches recognize a vocal range which accommodates a wide variety of characters- beyond his years! Bryan's voice moves easily from young and sweet to sassy, brash and heroic.
